Lasagna

Lasagna for 4 people

This lasagna features layers of tender lasagna noodles, a savory meat sauce made with ground beef and crushed tomatoes, and a creamy ricotta mixture. It’s topped with gooey melted mozzarella and Parmesan cheese, resulting in a comforting and satisfying dish. Bake it until golden and bubbly, then let it rest before serving. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Sheet
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Heat a large skillet over medium heat. Add the ground beef and onion, and cook until the beef is browned and the onion is soft.
  3. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Spread a thin layer of the meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Top with a layer of lasagna noodles, a layer of ricotta cheese, and a layer of mozzarella cheese. Repeat the layers until all the ingredients are used, ending with a layer of mozzarella cheese.
  5. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  6. Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
