Pizza

Classic Italian dish

Pizza is a classic Italian dish that features a thin crust topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings. It is baked in a hot oven until the crust is crispy and the cheese is melted and bubbly. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Mushrooms

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 450°F (230°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Spread tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a border around the edges.
  4. Top with mozzarella cheese, pepperoni, mushrooms, and any other toppings you like.
  5. Bake the pizza for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  6. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving. Enjoy!
