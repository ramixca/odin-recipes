Pupusa

Traditional Salvadoran dish

Pupusas are delicious Salvadoran corn cakes that are griddled and filled with various savory ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Masa harina
  • Water
  • Shredded cheese
  • Refried beans
  • Pork

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine masa harina and water to form a soft dough.
  2. Divide the dough into small balls and flatten them into discs.
  3. Fill each disc with your choice of filling, such as shredded cheese, refried beans, or pork.
  4. Seal the filling inside the dough and flatten the pupusa into a thick pancake shape.
  5. Cook the pupusas on a griddle until golden brown and crispy on the outside.
  6. Serve the pupusas hot with curtido, a tangy cabbage slaw, and salsa roja.
